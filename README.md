TITLE: WhatNext Vision Motors CRM System

A. WhatNext Vision Motors: Shaping the Future of Mobility with Innovation and Excellence
  > WhatNext Vision Motors is a Salesforce-based Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system designed to streamline vehicle ordering, dealer coordination, test-drive scheduling, service requests, and customer management. The system automates operational processes, ensures accurate stock handling, and enhances customer engagement through intelligent workflows and automated communication.

B. Overview
  > This project implements a fully customized Salesforce CRM solution for WhatNext Vision Motors, focused on improving customer experience and operational efficiency. It centralizes data for vehicles, dealers, customers, orders, test drives, and service requests, while using automation tools and Apex to ensure real-time accuracy and smooth business operations.
  > The system reduces manual work, prevents invalid orders, and delivers consistent customer communication.

C. Objectives
  1. Automate order assignment, stock validation, and test-drive reminders.
  2. Maintain accurate and consistent data across dealers, vehicles, and customer.
  3. Improve internal coordination with automated dealer assignment
  4. Provide better customer experience through timely updates and workflows
  5. Deliver real-time vehicle availability, order tracking, and service visibility

    2. Send scheduled test-drive reminders

    2. Deduct stock on confirmed orders
  c. Batch Apex
    1. Processes pending orders automatically
